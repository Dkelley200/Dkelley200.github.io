An Interview with Dianna Kelley.... Asked to describe herself, Dianna Kelley, BRM Systems Administrator smiles, eyes sparkling, and says, “I love the details of technology and maintaining our data systems but after work I ride motorcycles!” If the details of programming and the freedom of the road seem like an odd contrast one has only to talk with Dianna to discover a woman with a wide variety of interests and life experiences. An early work experience taught her to anticipate opportunities, a quality she continues to develop. Dianna’s nearly 20 year career path at BRM has allowed her to anticipate change and grow with it as the company grew. “I’m mechanically minded and naturally gravitated to the new technology,” she said. She was the sole tech support for the company, physically installing equipment, pulling lines, maintaining software and helping other users. “I was running full speed everyday. After our IT Director came on board to manage the computer systems I became part of our new Information Technology department. My job now focuses on making sure that our software gives us an accurate picture of our business development.”
